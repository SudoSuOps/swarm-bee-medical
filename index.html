The Problem

Cloud AI + patient data = compliance nightmare

Every major AI provider requires your data to leave your facility. GPT-4, Claude, Gemini — your patient records traverse third-party infrastructure. Third-party servers. Third-party employees. Third-party breach surfaces.

HIPAA requires you to know where protected health information lives at all times. State regulations are tightening. Patients are asking questions. The liability exposure of sending clinical data to a cloud LLM is not hypothetical — it's structural.

The answer isn't "don't use AI." The answer is sovereign AI: models that run on your hardware, trained on verified medical data, serving your clinicians from your rack. No cloud. No egress. No third-party risk.
